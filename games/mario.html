Overview

Join Mario and his new ally Cappy on a globe-trotting 3D platforming adventure to rescue Princess Peach from Bowser's wedding plans.

Key Features

  • Capture enemies and objects with Cappy to gain new abilities.
  • Explore massive sandbox kingdoms filled with secrets.
  • Co-op mode: a second player controls Cappy.
